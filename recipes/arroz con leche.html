Arroz con leche

Description

This delicious Mexican dessert is simple to make and a classic rice pudding recipe. If you love cinnamon and love pudding, you won't be able to resist this yummy treat.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice (1 cup)
  • Whole milk (8 cups)
  • Sugar (to taste)
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Raisins (1/2 cup)

Steps

  1. Pour some water in the pan and let it boil, add cinnamon and orange peel.
  2. Add rice and let it tenderize until it absorbs most of the water.
  3. Add cups of milk over time very gradually until rice gets soft and creamy.
  4. Add a teaspoon of salt. Add a spoonful of vanilla extract and sugar to taste. Stir in raisins.
  5. Take off the heat, let it cool down. Can pour cold milk if it gets too thick. Squeeze in some of the orange juice.
